Secondary Glazing Specialist is London's leading provider of secondary glazing solutions for listed buildings, conservation areas, and period properties. We specialize in preserving the architectural character of Victorian, Georgian, and Edwardian homes while dramatically improving thermal insulation and noise reduction.

Our expert team understands the complex planning permission requirements for heritage properties and delivers bespoke solutions that meet strict conservation regulations across London's most historic boroughs including Kensington & Chelsea, Westminster, Camden, and Richmond upon Thames.

Whether you own a Grade I, Grade II*, or Grade II listed building, or live in a conservation area, we provide sympathetic secondary glazing solutions that preserve original windows while reducing heat loss by up to 75% and blocking up to 80% of external noise pollution.

Our services include sash window secondary glazing, casement window solutions, bay window installations, and specialist acoustic glazing for properties near busy roads, railways, and flight paths. We serve all London boroughs and the Home Counties including Surrey, Kent, Essex, and Hertfordshire.
